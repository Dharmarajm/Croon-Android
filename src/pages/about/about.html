• Croon is a weekly competition app where people can upload or record audio/video clips (up to a minute long) of them singing, dancing, performing sports tricks or playing video game, etc.
  • Competitions will be a week long and contestants can upload as many videos or audio clips as they want in all genres until a specified time.
  • Once uploaded, contestants can share their uploads with all their friends and invite them to vote.
  • Registered users will be given an opportunity to vote (up to one vote per upload) for all their favorite uploads.
  • Contestants will be judged by other users based on the number of votes he/she gets. And the winners will be announced (for each genre) at the end of the week.
  • Croon will send daily updates on the current vote tally to all the registered users, contestants and voters.
  • If you want to watch your all time favorite uploads, Croon's history will have the past winners and uploads.
